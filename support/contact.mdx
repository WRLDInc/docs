## Contact Methods

### Email
Send us an email at [helpdesk@wrld.tech](mailto:helpdesk@wrld.tech)

### Support Portal
Submit a ticket at [wrld.support](https://wrld.support) or use the [Submit a Ticket](/support/submit-ticket) form directly from these docs.

### Client Portal
Sign in to manage your tickets and view SLA metrics at the [Client Portal](/support/client-login).

### Help Center
Browse our knowledge base at [wrld.help](https://wrld.help)

## Before Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue faster, please have the following ready:
- Your account email or client ID
- A clear description of the issue
- Any relevant error messages or screenshots
- Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable)

## Response Times

- **Critical Issues**: 1-4 hours
- **High Priority**: 4-8 hours
- **Standard Requests**: 24-48 hours
