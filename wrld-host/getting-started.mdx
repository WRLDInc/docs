## Get your site live

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [wrld.host](https://wrld.host) and sign up. If a WRLD team member is onboarding you, they may pre-provision your account — check your email for an invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Select shared, VPS, or dedicated hosting based on your needs. Unsure? Start with shared hosting — you can upgrade any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your domain">
    Point your domain's nameservers to WRLD.host, or [register a new domain](https://wrld.host/cart.php?a=add&domain=register) directly. For delegated access to an existing registrar account, see [GoDaddy Delegated Access](/onboarding/domains/godaddy-delegated-access).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy your site">
    Upload files via cPanel File Manager or FTP, use a one-click installer (WordPress, etc.), or connect your Git repository.
  </Step>
</Steps>
